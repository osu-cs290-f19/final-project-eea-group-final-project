Muscle Boosters!

Pre-Workout

Pre-workout is a supplement that is a multi-ingredient dietary formula designed to boost energy and athletic performance. They’re typically a powdered substance that you mix in water and drink before exercise. Many pre-workouts provide for more energy, enhanced focus, and increased muscle pumps.

Good brands: | C4 | Pre-Kaged | Six-Star | Ghost |

Creatine

Creatine is formed from amino acids and plays a role in converting food into energy. We get some creatine from our diets, mostly from meat and fish, and our bodies make the rest naturally. Creatine is able to enhance performance. Creatine provies energy to muscle cells to improve recovery between sets, which leads to rapid increases in muscle performance, size, and strength. Can be used both before and after workouts.

Protein Powder

If you go to a gym, you’ve probably heard the guys by the weight machines talking about the protein shakes they drink after a workout and what kind of shake they prefer. Protein powders can be made into a shake or consumed however you like You can buy protein powders in every nutrition store and all over the Internet. You can even find pre-mixed, ready-to-drink protein shakes in many stores
